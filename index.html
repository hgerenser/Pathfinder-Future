Frequently Asked Questions

Is this only for kids going into tech or certain careers?

No. Our programs are designed for any student (and their family) who wants to be prepared for the future. Whether you dream of becoming a doctor, artist, plumber or aren’t sure yet, the principles are the same: adaptability, curiosity and resilience.

How is Pathfinder Future different from school counseling?

Traditional counseling can be generic and stretched thin. We provide personalized, up‑to‑date guidance powered by AI insights. We also actively involve parents and offer coaching that schools usually can’t provide.

How much does it cost?

Program pricing varies based on the level of coaching and resources you choose. We offer options to fit different needs and budgets. Please check our Programs page or contact us for details.

Where can I learn more?

Explore our site to learn more about the Soft Skills Course, Pathfinder Bootcamp and our blog. You can also contact us directly with any questions.
